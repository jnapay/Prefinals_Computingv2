What is Generative AI?

Generative AI makes fresh stuff - like words, pictures, sounds, or clips - all by itself. Instead of just guessing or sorting things, it builds entirely new pieces. By studying tons of examples, it picks up trends then crafts its own versions. Think of it like copying vibes, not files. While old-school AI reacts, this kind dreams up output from scratch. Thanks to that skill, folks get more ways to share thoughts or tackle challenges.
